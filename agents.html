Agents

Valorant agents are unique characters, each equipped with special abilities and distinct roles. Duelists lead the charge with aggressive combat skills, Initiators disrupt enemy positions to create openings, Controllers manipulate the battlefield to control sightlines and movement, while Sentinels provide steadfast defense and crucial support. Together, these diverse agents offer a rich variety of playstyles and strategic teamwork opportunities, empowering players to outsmart opponents and secure victory in every round.
